Founder

Pawan Singh, owner of 3R Packers and Movers Satna, brings 10 years of corporate experience. In 2017, he launched the logistics company to address customer transportation issues. With a BCOM and MBA from NIA Mumbai, he ensures quality services in Madhya Pradesh and beyond.

The company offers diverse services, including bike and car transportation, household and office shifting. Starting modestly in 2017, 3R Packers and Movers Satna has expanded with branches in Satna, Rewa, Maihar, Katni, Panna, Sidhi, Singrauli, etc. Singh's rich interpretation company experience led to the establishment of the organization.
